The foundation of this analysis is built on multiple datasets spanning over 50 years of NASDAQ history, from which the following ingredients are of interest:

NASDAQ Stock Data

Historical price data for all NASDAQ-traded stocks collected using yfinance, covering the following:

  • Daily prices and volumes
  • Market capitalizations
  • Stock splits and adjustments
  • Listing and delisting dates

Our processed dataset contains ~10,970 listed stocks spanning 1972-2024.

Delisted Companies

In addition to active stocks, we also collected delisting information from CRSP (WRDS) for 17,231 delisted companies, including reasons for exit (bankruptcy, merger, regulatory violations).

Industry Classifications

For our sector analysis, we used GICS classifications from CRSP/Compustat and Yahoo Finance, achieving 96.3% coverage across 11 sectors and 215 sub-industries.
